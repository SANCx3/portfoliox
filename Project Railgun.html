Project Railgun

a follow up on my work on MazeGrappler, Project Railgun was created as a group project, my main role was focused on level design and art assets.

As Level designer, my priority was defining the flow statethat would define the way the game is defined through environmental interaction, object and enemy placement. I also had to layout a bit of a visual aesthetic to help people modify the textures of the level. part of my job as a level designer involves creating a skeleton for a world.
