Dash Cube 3d- the rough cut

I worked on this project alongside Project Cinder. While i did have a big hand in the artistic process of cinder, Dash Cube 3D is more me.

Dash Cube 3D aims to be an Open World 3D platformer about re-iterating on simplistic Ideas and game Mechanics in unity. The project itself was birthed from a desire to re-iterate on a older project,one I affectionatley call Dash Cube 2d. The game follows Dash Cube, an ordinary cube who washes ashore on an island inhabited by lots of shapes after trying to get his prized gaming handheld back. while initially intended to be a one off project, my unfamiliarity with the unity game engine led me to refocusing my efforts to making this game a more long term project. this re-evaluation has shown me how much i've grown and how much i've still got to improve within Unity and Blender, as I had to learn alot of new skills such as texture painting and C# in order to even make what I've built.

While very simplistic and barebones, I enjoyed the process of making this game and look forward to making more progress and changes.
